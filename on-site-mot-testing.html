More than a box-tick

An MOT at Bromspec is more than a legal formality. Your vehicle is MOT tested on-site at our Lopcombe workshop by testers who understand exactly what they're looking at — so genuine issues are spotted early, explained in plain English, and never inflated into work you don't need.

We're BMW & MINI specialists, but all makes are welcome to the same standard. Book a while-you-wait slot, or take one of our free courtesy cars and get on with your day while we take care of it.

  • Class 4 MOT testing carried out on-site
  • Express ‘while you wait’ or a courtesy car for the day
  • Honest advice — no pressure, no unnecessary work
  • 10% off labour for Blue Light Card holders

What happens if it fails?

If your car needs work to pass, you're already in the right place. Because we service and repair these vehicles every day, failure items can usually be quoted and put right on the spot — using genuine or OE-quality parts, with interest-free finance available to spread the cost. You'll always get a clear explanation and a price before any work begins.
