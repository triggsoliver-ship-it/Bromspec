Clear the blockage, keep the filter

A blocked DPF warning light, loss of power or limp mode usually means soot and ash have built up faster than your car can burn off. Left alone, that ends in an expensive new filter. Our DPF clean removes up to 100% of the carbon, soot and ash — restoring flow and getting your car running properly again, often within a matter of hours.

We don't just clean and send you on your way. We check why it blocked in the first place, so the problem doesn't simply return.

  • Removes up to 100% of soot, carbon and ash
  • Restores flow and clears blocked-DPF warnings
  • A fraction of the cost of a new filter
  • Root cause checked with full diagnostics
images/service-servicing.jpg

Why filters block

Lots of short, low-speed journeys stop the DPF reaching the temperature it needs to regenerate. Faults in the EGR, sensors or fuel system make it worse. Because we combine cleaning with proper diagnostics, we fix the cause — not just the symptom — so you're not back in a few months' time.
