Stay mobile while we work

We understand that being without a car is a real inconvenience, so we've invested in a fleet of courtesy vehicles for our customers to use. Whether your car is in for a service, an MOT or a bigger repair, you can drop it off, take a courtesy car and carry on with work, the school run or whatever your day holds.

Courtesy cars are free of charge and available with advance booking, subject to availability — so it's always worth mentioning when you book so we can reserve one for you.

  • Free of charge for booked-in customers
  • A fleet of BMW & MINI courtesy vehicles
  • Ideal for services, MOTs and longer repairs
  • Reserve yours in advance, subject to availability

Just ask when you book

Because demand is high, a quick call ahead is the best way to make sure a courtesy car is ready and waiting on the day of your appointment. Let us know your dates and we'll do the rest.
