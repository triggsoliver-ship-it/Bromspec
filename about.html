One thing, done properly

We focus on delivering the highest standard of BMW and MINI specialist care. Every car that comes through our workshop is handled by experienced technicians using factory-level diagnostic tools, systems and procedures — exactly as BMW intended.

That’s why owners travel to us from Salisbury, Winchester, Andover, Amesbury and across Wiltshire. They know their car is in safe, expert hands, their time is respected, and every recommendation is clear, honest and considered. With over 75 years of combined BMW and MINI specialist experience, we cover everything from routine MOTs and servicing to advanced diagnostics and technical work.
