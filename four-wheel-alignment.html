Straighten up and save your tyres

Hit a kerb or a pothole, fitted new tyres, or noticed the car pulling to one side? Incorrect wheel alignment causes uneven tyre wear, vibration and heavier steering — and quietly costs you money in fuel and premature tyre replacement.

We measure and adjust your steering geometry to the manufacturer's specification, so your car tracks straight, steers cleanly and wears its tyres evenly. It's one of the most cost-effective jobs you can do for a smoother, safer drive.

  • Precision four-wheel alignment to manufacturer spec
  • Even tyre wear and longer tyre life
  • Sharper handling and reduced vibration
  • Better fuel economy and shorter braking distance

Do it alongside your service

Alignment pairs perfectly with a service, new tyres or suspension work. Book it together and we'll check everything in one visit — with honest advice on whether an adjustment is actually needed.
