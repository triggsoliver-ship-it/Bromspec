Servicing that protects your car — and its value

From an interim oil service to a full manufacturer schedule, we use approved procedures, diagnostics and high-quality parts to keep your car reliable, efficient and safe. Preventative maintenance now avoids the costly repairs later — and a properly stamped service history protects your car's resale value.

BMW and MINI are our speciality, but every make is serviced to the same standard, without affecting your manufacturer warranty. Courtesy cars keep you moving, and interest-free finance is available to spread the cost.

  • Manufacturer-level servicing to schedule
  • Preventative maintenance to avoid costly repairs
  • Service history kept up to date
  • Warranty-friendly — all makes welcome
