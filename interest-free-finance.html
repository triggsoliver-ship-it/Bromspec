Repairs shouldn't wait for payday

Unexpected bills happen. Through our partnership with Payment Assist, we can offer 4 months of interest-free credit on your servicing and repair bills — so you can get the work done now and pay in manageable instalments, with no interest to worry about.

The application is quick and simple, most decisions are instant, and there's no reason to put off the work your car needs. Just ask us about finance when you book.

  • 4 months interest-free credit via Payment Assist
  • Spread the cost of servicing and repairs
  • Quick, simple application with fast decisions
  • No need to delay essential work

Simple, transparent, no catches

We'll always give you a clear price for the work first. If you'd like to spread it, we'll walk you through the interest-free option there and then — you stay in control, with no pressure and no hidden costs. Finance is subject to status and a simple application.
